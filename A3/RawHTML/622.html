Music Download: SHARONEE – KADOSH | @sharonee_muzik

Xtrem GospelMarch 5, 2016
EntertainmentMusic11 views 0
Everyth‎ing that God is, is wrapped up in one word ‘Holy’. This is why the angels do not say to him, you are good, merciful, loving,awesome, glorious eternity,etc. They cry Holy! Holy! Holy!
Kadosh is the Hebrew word for Holy, my love and appreciation for the depth of the Hebrew language made me write this song in Hebrew‎.
With the sea of angels worshipping Eloah non-stop, he still prizes and longs for our worship, let’s join the heavenly choir in the beauty of holiness to call Him KADOSH.
