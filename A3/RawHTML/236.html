    Mogck, Davies girls top Minot in hockey

    By Forum staff reports on Jan 29, 2017 at 3:36 p.m.

    MINOT, N.D.—Annika Mogck scored two goals to help lift Fargo Davies to a 5-2 high school girls hockey win at Minot Saturday.

    Mogck scored in the first and third periods. Reilly Mach, Lauren Hanson and Alexis Sampson also scored for Davies. Davies' goalie Sydney Peterson had 27 saves.

    Fargo Davies improves to 13-5 overall with the win. Williston falls to 8-9 overall.
