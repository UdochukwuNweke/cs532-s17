Music and Art for animals

Lefty's hosts benefit to raise money

By: Ashley Carrasco

Posted: Jan 29, 2017 07:51 PM CST

Updated: Jan 29, 2017 08:30 PM CST

DES MOINES - Lefty's in Des Moines hosted a benefit for the Animal Rescue League of Iowa Sunday afternoon. The event featured a raffle with an indoor art market and full bar.
As local 5's photojournalist Griffin Wright shows us, it's all about helping out local pets.

On top of the public's donation, any pink poodle or lefty bomb ordered, lefty's donated $1.00 to the ARL.
