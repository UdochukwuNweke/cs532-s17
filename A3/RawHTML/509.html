2016 All-Texomaland Football Class 4A-5A

Sunday
Jan 29, 2017 at 12:01 AM

Herald Democrat

Here is the All-Texomaland Class 4A-5A Football team for the 2016 season:


Most Valuable Player – Tre’ Lyday, Sr., Denison.


Offensive Player of the Year – Nathaniel Omayebu, Jr., Sherman.


Defensive Player of the Year – Taivian Rucker, Sr., Denison.


Newcomers of the Year – Kaedric Cobbs, Fr., Melissa; Ja’Bray Young, Fr., Melissa.


Coach of the Year – Seth Stinton, Melissa.


First team


Offense


Quarterback – David Bedgood, Sr., Sherman.


Running backs – John Franklin, Sr., Celina; Daunte Rose, Sr., Anna; Calion Baker, Jr., Gainesville.


Wide receivers – Tylor Jones, Jr., Sherman; Steven Lee, Sr., Anna.


Tight End – Gunnar Murphy, Sr., Melissa.


Offensive Line – Antoine Robison, Sr., Denison; Josh Schnitker, Sr., Sherman; Justin Nichols, Sr., Sherman; Hayden Reed, Sr., Celina; Luke Melton, Sr., Celina.


Kicker – Pedro Salvador, Sr., Sherman.


Defense


Defensive ends – Jose Camacho, Sr., Celina; Jonathan Uland, Sr., Bonham.


Defensive tackles – Jacob Shelton, Sr., Melissa; Tyler Eddington, Jr., Gainesville.


Linebackers – Hunter Bigelow, Sr., Denison; Jaylin Cotton, Jr., Sherman; Hayden Shortes, Sr., Celina; Evan Wilson, Sr., Bonham.


Defensive backs – Brayson Savage, Sr., Sherman; Decurious Smith, Soph., Denison; Andre Norman, Sr., Celina; Keelan Crosby, Jr., Anna.


Punter – Austin Simmerman, Sr. Bonham.


Second team


Offense


Quarterbacks – Conner Pingleton, Sr., Celina; Tate Whittington, Jr., Melissa.


Running backs – Brent Wilson, Jr., Sherman; Kenny Gaffney, Jr., Denison; Xzavion Epps, Sr., Bonham.


Wide receivers – Zach Sanders, Sr., Denison; Daishawn Nelson, Sr., Sherman.


Offensive Line – Hayden Hayes, Sr., Sherman; Brakeilon Powell, Sr., Denison; Julien Landin, Sr., Denison; Dillon Rollins, Sr., Anna; Dante Brooks, Sr., Melissa.


Tight end – Janson Robeson, Sr., Gainesville.


Kickers – David Sauceda, Sr., Denison; Eduardo Paulino, Sr., Celina.


Defense


Defensive ends – Kentrell Mathenia, Sr., Denison; Josiah Avery, Jr., Anna; Sam Snyder, Sr., Celina.


Defensive tackles – Jared Reeves, Sr., Celina; T.K. Walton, Soph, Sherman.


Linebackers – Devante Clayton, Jr., Sherman; Cade Gordy, Soph., Denison; Seth Bennett, Sr., Gainesville; Antonio Barragan, Sr., Anna; Tarek Wallace, Sr., Melissa.


Defensive backs – Khalil Finley, Sr., Denison; Cole McGraw, Jr., Melissa; Trevor Hartwell, Jr., Bonham; Miles Teague, Sr., Celina.


Punter – Hunter Turbeville, Sr., Gainesville.
