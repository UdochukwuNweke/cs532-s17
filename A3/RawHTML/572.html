Huh Gak releases ‘rolling music’ teaser for upcoming mini album ‘Love Letter’

January 30, 2017

Huh Gak has released a ‘rolling music’ teaser for his 5th mini album ‘Love Letter’!

The teaser showcases all 6 songs on his mini album, including title song “Miss You”. As usual, he showcases his voice perfectly in emotional ballads and R&Bs, and this is sure to be another great release for fans to listen to in the cold weather.

Check out his teaser above!
