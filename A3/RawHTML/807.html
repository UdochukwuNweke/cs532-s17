Pat Tillman’s Wife Blasts Trump’s Muslim Ban on Facebook

By Nasheena Davis -
January 29, 2017
2
809
Share on Facebook
Tweet on Twitter
  • tweet

Donald Trump’s Muslim Ban is being condemned far and wide. American hero and former NFL player Pat Tillman’s widow Marie took to Facebook to blast the president and the controversial ban.

Tillman left behind millions in the NFL to serve his country as a member of the Army Rangers after the 9/11 attacks. He was killed while in service in Afghanistan in 2004. While Trump is selling the ban on Muslim immigration as a precaution to preserve the nation’s safety, a number of servicemen and women have spoken against it.
