#Results: WCPW “Edinburgh” *FULL Results*

Date: January 30, 2017Author: Hashtag Wrest 0 Comments

Date: 29/1/2017
Promotion: What Culture Pro Wrestling
Event: Edinburgh
Venue: Meadowbank Sports Centre, Edinburgh
Source: OSWtv

Match Results…

Dark match… Prince Ameen def. Doug Williams

WCPW Tag Team Champions, Moss X Slater (Johnny Moss & Liam Slater) def. Lou King Sharp & Kid Fite

Following the match, Moss X Slater issued an Open Challenge for “True Destiny”

BT Gunn def. Gabriel Kidd

#1 Contenders match
Joe Coffey def. Moose

ROH World Championship Three-Way match
Champion, Adam Cole def. El Ligero & Jay Lethal

Handicap match
Rampage def. Drake & Prospect (Lucas Archer & Alex Gracie)

Martin Kirby def. Drake

Bea Priestley def. Little Miss Roxxy

WCPW World Championship match
Champion, Drew Galloway def. Joe Hendry
