What's affinitweet? 
Affinitweet is based on the Twitter API and used public information from two
users (you and another Twitter user) to check their #affinitwees .
Statistics and common points are calculated from these accounts in order to show you your results.
You can easily check your #affinitwees with anyone on Twitter (except private accounts).
