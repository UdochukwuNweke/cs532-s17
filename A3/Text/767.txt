Billionaire industrialists’ organization didn’t support Trump in 2016 race 
By 
Rebecca Ballhaus 
Rebecca Ballhaus 
The Wall Street Journal 
Biography Rebecca Ballhaus 
@rebeccaballhaus 
rebecca.ballhaus@wsj.com 
Updated Jan. 29, 2017 9:23 p.m. ET
