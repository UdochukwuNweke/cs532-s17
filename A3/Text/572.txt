Huh Gak releases &#8216;rolling music&#8217; teaser for upcoming mini album &#8216;Love Letter&#8217; 			 
January 30, 2017 		 		
Huh Gak has released a &#8216;rolling music&#8217; teaser for his 5th mini album &#8216;Love Letter&#8217;! 
The teaser showcases all 6 songs on his mini album, including title song &#8220;Miss You&#8221;. As usual, he showcases his voice perfectly in emotional ballads and R&amp;Bs, and this is sure to be another great release for fans to listen to in the cold weather.
