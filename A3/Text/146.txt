This week’s youth calendar (Photo: Getty Images/iStockphoto)    CONNECT  TWEET  LINKEDIN  COMMENT  EMAIL  MORE  To submit an event for the Youth Calendar, email sports@greenbaypressgazette.com. Include your event’s day and date, place, fees, details, contact person, phone number, email and website addresses. BASEBALL FEB. 3-24: Fungo Fridays, at Players Choice in Appleton. Coaches hit ground balls for an hour on a full-size infield for ages 9-17. Dates are Feb. 3, 17 and 24 from 5-6 p.m., each session is $10. More info: www.pcfoxcities.com. FEB. 4: Hitting camp, at Players Choice, 10:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m.. Focus is on everything from approach, grip, stance, load, bat path, contact and finish of the swing. Cost is $45. More info: www.pcfoxcities.com. BASKETBALL JAN. 30: Midwest Force Select AAU girls teams seeks players for its 12u (sixth grade) and 11u (fifth grade) teams. More info: www.midwestforceselect.com/. FEB. 12-MARCH 5: The Legends girls program is holding tryouts at Foxview Intermediate School in De Pere. Tryouts for Grade 7 is Feb. 12, 3:30-5:30 p.m., Grade 6 is Feb. 12, 6-8 p.m., Grade 5 is Feb. 19 from 3:30 p.m. and Grade 8, Feb. 19 from 6-8 p.m. Grade 9 is March 5 from 3:30-5:30 p.m., and Grades 10-11 is March 5 from 6-8 p.m. More information can be found at legendsbasketball.org or by contacting Kris Johnson, (920) 562-2483 or krisjohnson32@yahoo.com. FEB. 12-MARCH 12: The Purple Aces girls basketball program is hosting team tryouts, at Valley View school in Ashwaubenon. Tryout times: 13u (7th grade), Feb. 12, 4:30-5:45 p.m.; 14u (8th grade), Feb. 12, 6-7:15 p.m.; 11u (5th grade), Feb. 19, 5:30-7 p.m.; 12u (6th grade), Feb. 26, 5:30-7 p.m.; 15u (freshmen), March 12, 4:30-5:45 p.m.; 16u (sophomores), March 12, 6-7:15 p.m.; 17u (junios), 6-7:15 p.m. Cost is $10 to try out. More info: www.purpleacesgb.com. MARCH 4-5: Peshtigo Girls Hoops Club tournaments. Tournaments for grades 4, 5 and 6 is March 4, for grades 3, 7 and 8 is March 5. Girls on team must be from same school district. Fee is $160 with three-game guarantee. For more info or to register, contact Kevin Harris at kharris13@new.rr.com or (715) 801-0060. SOFTBALL FEB. 2, 9, 16: Pitching instruction at Players Choice in Appleton, from 5-6 p.m. on Feb. 2, 9 and 16. Event is open to girls ages 8-11 and costs $75. More info: www.pcplayerschoice.com. FEB. 12-19: St. Norbert softball fundamentals camps at the Sports Emporium in De Pere. Grades 8-12 is Feb. 12 from 9:30-10:45 a.m. (pitching and catching) and 10:45-11:30 a.m. (defense/fielding) and Feb. 19, 10:30-11:30 a.m. (hitting). Cost is $40 for one session, $75 for two or $110 for three. For more info, contact JoAnn Krueger, (920) 619-6237 or joann.krueger@snc.edu or visit www.sportsemporiuimgb.com. MARCH 3-5: Panther Elite Softball’s inaugural indoor tournament, at will Players Choice of the Fox Cities. The tournament will have a 10U and 11U bracket, with a three-game guarantee. Cost is $250 per team. Sign up at www.pantherelitesoftball.com/tournaments. APRIL 15: The Green Bay Optimist Club is now accepting registrations for the 2017 summer softball program for girls in grades 4k through 9. Games are played at the Finger Road Ball Complex during June and July. Registration forms have already been sent to the public schools for distribution.
