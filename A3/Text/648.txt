   Jan 19, 2017  Share Tweet    Image via Getty/Prince Williams  You may not know Breyan Isaac&apos;s name, but you&apos;ve heard his music. Over the past five years or so, the 36-year-old songwriter and producer has written massive hits for artists like Flo Rida, Kesha, and Charlie Puth. Now, in a new interview with&#xA0; Songwriter Universe , Isaac reveals that he has written one song that is sure to make hip-hop fans go nuts.&#xA0; Towards the end of the interview, Isaac is asked if he has any upcoming records he&apos;s excited about, and he lets fly some pretty big news: 
There&#x2019;s a song coming out with Lil Uzi Vert, Travis Scott and Quavo (from the group, Migos) that&#x2019;s going to be the theme song for&#xA0; Fast &amp; Furious 8 . That will be a single out in March, or close to when the movie comes out in April.&#xA0; 
&#xA0;As if&#xA0; The Fate of the Furious &#xA0; (the movie&apos;s actual title) needed any more anticipation&#x2014;even the film&apos;s trailer is setting all kinds of records . The film&apos;s star Vin Diesel even predicted that it will win Oscars.&#xA0;  Related Jason Statham Says He&#x27;s Coming Back For &#x27;Furious 8&#x27; Listen to Quavo&#x27;s &quot;Trapper Girl&quot; f/ Young Thug and Peewee Longway  Listen to Young Thug&#x27;s &#x27;Slime Season 3&#x27; Single &quot;F Cancer&quot; f/ Quavo  The as-yet-untitled track&apos;s release will mark the first time that Quavo, Vert, and Scott have all appeared together, but they have joined forces before in various combinations. Scott and Quavo&#xA0;are both on &quot;Pick Up the Phone&quot; with Young Thug. and Scott and Vert&#xA0;dueted on &quot;Ra Ra.&quot; &#xA0;Scott and Quavo even recently teased a joint album . The Fate of the Furious hits theaters on April 16.
